What is Percentage ?

A Percentage is a number or ratio expressed as a fraction of 100. It is often denoted using the percent sign, "%"


Percentage Formula

Find What % is x of y ?
Percentage can be calculated by dividing the number with principal number and mutiplying with 100. So, Percentage Formula, p = (x/y)*100

Examples:
p = (x/y)*100 = (10/100)*100 = 10%
p = (x/y)*100 = (25.5/100)*100 = 25.5%
p = (x/y)*100 = (100/400)*100 = 25%
